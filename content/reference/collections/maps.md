# Maps

In this wiki, a **map** is not only a geographic chart: it is any **structured index** that shows how big themes connect. Use the pages below as entry points when you want breadth before depth.

## Discipline and topic hubs

- [[Philosophy]] — traditions, problems, and named thinkers.
- [[Culture]] — arts, media, places, and everyday cultural themes.
- [[Art]] — visual art and related practice.
- [[Science]] — empirical topics and methods-oriented notes.
- [[Social Science]] — institutions, politics, economics, and collective behavior.

## How to use this page

When a note feels isolated, add a link **up** to a hub (e.g. [[Culture]]) and **sideways** to a sibling (e.g. [[Movies]] next to [[Poetry]]). That keeps the graph navigable without maintaining one giant outline.
