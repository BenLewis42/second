# Dharani

Thoughts are peace words are peace work is peace

I honor the interconnectedness of all beings, embracing the shared essence beyond the illusion of separateness. Releasing the shadows of comparison, I recognize the Buddha nature within each of us. With a heart free from judgment, I see the intrinsic worth in every soul, knowing the ego's impermanence. My compassion flows through my caring heart, adaptability, and resilience, woven from the fabric of our collective existence. I nurture inner peace and extend boundless loving-kindness to all beings, embracing the fleeting nature of self.
