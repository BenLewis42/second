# Culture

**Culture** is used here in the anthropological and everyday sense: shared symbols, practices, tastes, and institutions that shape how people live together—not “high culture” only, and not a single national story.

This site splits culture across several folders so files stay findable: **art**, **film**, **literature**, and **topics** for cross-cutting themes (places, fashion, games, journalism, and the like). If you are editing in the repo, see `docs/TAXONOMY.md` for where new notes should live.
