# sotce

thank you for another day

---

I try to feel it until completion, i create the conditions that allow me to do this. I usually need to be alone to listen to myself. When i'm patient, silence holds all the answers.

A perfect rebirth in the pure lands seems so beyond me down on this here sand where everything seems opaque and dense and so complex. So why was I gifted with knowing what I know or at least sensing delicately and intimately the delicious vibe that is just beyond our reach and comprehension, why was I gifted with this knowing and then left to fight with dogs in my head.

One person holds the leash so that others can freak out.
One person stays up to kiss everyone goodnight

---

Everyone's mind tells stories. We tell ourselves stories in order to live, in order to live with ourselves. In fact any time you step away from your life, any time you are in the past or the future or planning or deciding or looking around this is a story. Any time you step out of the precious present narrative you are a storyteller. In this truth resounds my greatest fear, or one of them, that I am lying to myself.

He who will marry me some day or block me watched me taking the contacts out, or failing to, later on. He was kind of annoyed by it.

I've put my finger on the final hour that this task could possibly be born into. The task of writing something right now.

the garden of my heart

I say something good. I present that thing in me that doesn't move or change. That thing that cannot be reckoned with. That thing which is not interested in vanity or pride. That thing I wish I was closer with, that I only meet these days from getting hurt or getting tired or running hard. That thing which begs to be free, which yearns towards God and only towards God like how a moth moves towards light. That thing which only sees the divine.

it feels familiar like a childhood dream.

To me an item poses a responsibility and a burden. I will unwrap it and dispose of its wrappings and collapse its box and then it sits in my room or in my bathroom and watches me until I use it or I give it away.

Only strangers travel. I think I've grown quite strange.

I am completely in charge of my belongings, that they must serve their purpose, that they will follow me endlessly unless I murder them.

In a simple room I am clear minded, my senses rest in basic space

Shit is a strong word I'm sure, there are things in that suitcase that I really treasure, but I can't really think of anything in particular

clothes that would spend more time being picked up and shoved somewhere than anything else.

It's fine to do these things, and maybe you like these things more than I do, but we have been set up to do things like this since the day we were born. We have been set up to wade through routines that aren't serving us. So be firm in what you want, it's much more delicious to save up for a big joy than to spend the summer doing half hearted things with people from your hometown.

one small thing my life was burdened by not having

I started to relax and think about it all a bit less and then I was slowly buying people coffees and birthday presents and picking up tabs.

it's wonderful to feel lucky. Grateful without the need to prove anything or attach anywhere, just grateful

I feel lucky to have books and clean water at the touch of a button. I am lucky to have beautiful hands and unlimited drawing supplies. I am lucky that I can walk and breathe and listen closely. A lot of times I feel like the luckiest girl in the world. Are you getting the game? I usually play it every day.

Maybe every fortune brings its weight in grief. Maybe your life comes with a unique set of challenges that I cannot fathom.

Do remember, everything you have and don't have, everything that you define yourself by and all that has come to be in your orb is nothing more than a rainbow settled upon the six senses. As much as you are a girl or a swimmer or a blonde (making stuff up here) this inheritance has no size or shape or color. It has no rhyme or reason and it could vanish in an instant. it has nothing to do with you, it is a quality imposed on your experience of life, it is an invisible pearl in your hand. It is a random chunk of money. Am I making any sense?

I think there is very little to think about. If it is feeling heavy on a particular day then you might want to try to be kind and humble and generous about this quality. You might share your luck when it feels lucky without worrying too much about it, because worry and control are emanations of fear. And fear of what?

remember that life is a dream. This is what I wish for you

it ran to me like a little child

a dimension of sweetness that i hadn't thought to miss before.

the mind has a funny way of protecting us from ourselves, protecting us from the subjugation of a sudden and shattering love

always open and soft

something most adults don't get out of one another.

to appear in front of someone as bare as a fetus and to be had like that. this wild inside part of me, this unfamiliar depth lept out and roared at the remembrance, perhaps longing to be recognized, perhaps, longing to be dragged into the light.

sometimes i cry because these words are all i have to explain myself. i hope i am able to reach you.

(on binge eating)
This time, like any other, is important. You are becoming who you are. All I can ask of you is to be brave and face your pain, look right at it, go deep and understand it. I think the most you can make of this is knowing it as well as you can. as you deepen your experience of yourself you will deepen in your wisdom and your compassion for others. it will end when it's ready to, but there's something you need to see to here.
Other than that, be kind with yourself, understand what you've been through and that space and sigh and pause is so necessary for movement and growth. Hydrate and eat and sleep in clean sheets.

*Sotce, I don't like eating. It's often a burden to my lightness.*
It is so easy to long to be something other than what you are. But do try to remember what you are. You are many things i'm sure but most literally you are in the body of a human girl who is very much tied to this earth. Our tiedness is a lot of what gives us shape and dimension; it is the way we love others, what we place in their hands, it is the choices we make that stack into months and years, it is how we choose to spend our precious lives. What holds you to this earth is what you eat. This is an ancient biological agreement we've made with the trees. that we will eat and we will eat to complete cycles and we will live here. In this way eating is your birthright. It can feel exhilarating to push those limits, I know this, but in my opinion it is not a very interesting use of your time here. I'm not sure exactly what your work is on earth but you and I both know that there is a lot to do. so i hope you will give yourself fuel to complete your missions and I hope you will take up space and i think that even if the path forward isn't so straight and simple you will build a beautiful life.

an inside joke with myself

The clocks go clockwise and the planets go counter clockwise

relying on her to release my turn to board.

Something familiar and compounded. Something they give you because they love you.

I was high off in the mountains closing in on his guru

a half dream I can see the edges of.

I tell him about his world with the authority, certainty, plainness of seeing it. I tell him to do what he likes, that it doesn't matter what he does so much as how he does it, that his role stays the same throughout the rooms he inhabits and that his mission in life is more about brotherhood, kinship, filial piety, than anything else, waiting his turn, following through. Courage and decency. Bravery and kindness. That he should go to his sister's wedding. There is not a single errant tenant between us.
He puts his fingertips on my eyelids and prays in a murmur, in his language that I've heard before and cannot understand.

He wakes me when it's time to land and the vibe is that we can't be friends once we join the others. That he has to act right for his people. I am glad I saw him as God intended because I won't him again.

I wanted to be an ex girlfriend again before i went to be a mental health write off.

Normal is tossed around a lot as a label, and i know I'm a culprit of using this term to differentiate between you and me, or between us and them. But i don't really see people like that. More so i see people as real and unreal. Some existences just don't click in my mind. With a real person i can see their heart quite plainly. I am in love with them in a sense, and i can picture them as a little kid. I see their realness and i want to give them fruit and stuffed animals. With an unreal person i don't get how it all adds up. I want to tap on their forehead and ask if anyone is home. Somewhere in me knows that this type of person is just under a lot of spells. And me drawing this distinction in character just means i am not enlightened. I am living walking proof of this.

I don't think its a straight line though, to getting there, i see it more as a spiral with a white light in the center. The white light is enlightenment, clear eyed view, perfect loving mind, no thinking just bountiful compassion and wisdom. That sort of thing. Gorgeous nature. Effortless and endless and complete and unchanging, it does not flicker, it is a steady current that doesn't come from eating mushrooms and it doesn't come from falling in love.

everything we had between us was too precious to be felt or shared. A lot of meeting goes like this. We meet to say goodbye. We feel good saying it, even. No is a closed circle, a full sentence.

On the paper i drew one of my comics, the bunny and the dog sitting on a bench. The dog says "i could never understand you" and the bunny says "believe me, you understood me. You understood me and it changed my life."
I folded the slip of paper in two and returned it to the moon faced man. He opened it and looked at my drawing, he touched his face and nodded slowly. He had nothing to say so he made that frowny smiling face and kept nodding. Nice… Nice. Very nice. Very real. Real girl. Not normal. Something else. Niceeeee

A bee lives for about forty days and in that lifetime it usually visits over a thousand flowers.
