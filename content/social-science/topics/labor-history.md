## Silent Agitators

**Silent agitators** (also called silent organizers and stickerettes) are stickers used by the Industrial Workers of the World (IWW).

The IWW publication *Industrial Worker* of Spokane, Washington, advertised stickers as early as April 20, 1911. Silent agitators were produced by the millions.
