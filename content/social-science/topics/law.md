## Preemption

A legal doctrine that allows a higher level of government to limit or even eliminate the power of a lower level of government to regulate a specific issue.

## Public-Function Test

A legal principle used to determine whether a private entity is performing a function that is traditionally and exclusively done by the government, and therefore should be subject to the same constitutional constraints as a government entity. This test is primarily applied in the context of the Fourteenth Amendment to the United States Constitution, which prohibits states from denying any person equal protection under the law.
