## Legitimation Crisis

Refers to a decline in the confidence of administrative functions, institutions, or leadership. An institution or organization does not have the administrative capabilities to maintain or establish structures effective in achieving their end goals.

A state is perceived as being **legitimate** when its citizens treat it as properly holding and exercising political power. When political actors engage in the process of legitimation they are pursuing legitimacy for themselves or for another institution.
