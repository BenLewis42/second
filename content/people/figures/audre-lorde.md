## Response to Descartes

The American writer, professor, philosopher, intersectional feminist and civil rights activist [Audre Lorde](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Audre_Lorde) responded that while 'The white fathers told us: I think, therefore I am. The black mother within each of us—the poet—whispers in our dreams: I feel, therefore I can be free', thus emphasising emotion and feeling as another important determiner of truth and our shared humanity.
