- Eat your last big meal 3–4 hours before bed time.
- For that last meal, don't make it acidic (e.g., tomato-based pasta sauce), and limit the sodium.
- Drink your last caffeinated beverage before noon.
- Take a warm shower within an hour of bedtime.
- Optional snacks that improve sleep (both anecdotally and based on studies): refrigerated (not canned) sauerkraut, raisins, and almonds. Apart from fermented foods being great for brain health, the other things have melatonin, serotonin, magnesium, and potassium which all contribute to calming, lowering blood pressure, and setting yourself up for a good night's rest.
- Drink 4oz of water before bed.
