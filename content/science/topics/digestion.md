# Digestion

---

Avoid:

1. Sweeteners
2. Nicotine
3. High FODMAP food
4. Fruits: Apples, pears, mangoes, watermelon, and other stone fruits (peaches, plums, cherries). Dried fruits and fruit juice concentrate, avocados, figs, and apricots.
5. Vegetables: Garlic and onions, including garlic powder and onion powder. Asparagus, artichokes, broccoli, Brussels sprouts, cauliflower, and leeks. Cabbage, mushrooms, peas, and some types of squash.
6. Grains: Wheat, rye and barley
7. Dairy: Lactose
8. Legumes: Beans (baked beans, black beans, kidney beans, etc.), lentils, and chickpeas
