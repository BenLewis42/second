## Eugeroics

A class of drugs that promote wakefulness and alertness. They are also known as psychotropic drugs, and are used to treat narcolepsy and other sleep disorders. Eugeroics are relatively non-addictive, and reduce tiredness, drowsiness, and the need for sleep.

## Anxiolytic

A medication or other intervention that reduces anxiety.

**Anxiogenic:** increase anxiety.
